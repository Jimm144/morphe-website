What is MicroG?

Google Services

MicroG provides Google Play Services support, enabling the use of your Google account with YouTube Morphe and YT Music Morphe.

Privacy Focused

MicroG is an open-source reimplementation of Google services that respects your privacy while providing essential functionality.

One-Time Install

Install MicroG once and it works with all your Morphe apps. No need to reinstall when updating YouTube Morphe and YT Music Morphe.

Morphe+

A community fork of Morphe — next generation Android app patching

Morphe does not add analytics or data collection to any patched apps.
